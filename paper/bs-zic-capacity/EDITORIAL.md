# Editorial assessment (claude-fable-5, 2026-07-19)

**Verdict: not suitable for IEEE Transactions on Information Theory submission.**

The mathematics is correct (audited twice), but the content is classical: the
strong regime instantiates Sato / Costa–El Gamal strong-interference machinery,
and the weak regime's closed form follows from the capacity-region equivalence
to Benzel (1979) stated by Liu–Goldsmith (2009). A Transactions referee would
reject on novelty, correctly.

**Recommended disposition:** keep as an expository repository note (its current
honest framing), optionally post to arXiv as a self-contained exposition — the
eight-line Mrs. Gerber converse and the two-line achievability identity are a
genuinely clean write-up of a scattered classical result — but do not submit to
a journal. The pedagogical value and the demonstration of the multi-agent
review pipeline are the note's real contributions.
